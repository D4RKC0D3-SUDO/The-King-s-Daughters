The Path of the Chosen Woman

Equipping women ministers through a 10-week journey of prayer, discipleship, and leadership.

Program Overview

This mentorship program is designed to raise and equip women ministers with biblical leadership, prayer, and ministry skills. Over 10 weeks, participants will be guided through teaching, mentorship, and practical ministry development.

Topics Covered

  • Understanding God’s Call
  • Prayer & Intercession
  • Biblical Leadership
  • Discipleship & Mentorship
  • Family Restoration
  • Servant Leadership
  • Unity in the Body of Christ
  • Integrity & Holiness
  • Strategic Ministry Skills
  • Commissioning & Sending

Program Fees

Individual: 15,000 Ksh

Group (5+ women): 10,000 Ksh per person
