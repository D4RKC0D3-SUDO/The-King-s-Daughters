The Path of the Chosen Woman

Embark on a transformative 10‑week journey designed to equip women ministers with prayer, discipleship, and leadership skills for Kingdom impact.

Program Overview

The Mentorship Program is a unique discipleship pathway crafted to raise Spirit‑filled women leaders across Africa and beyond. Over ten weeks, participants will be immersed in biblical teaching, spiritual mentorship, and practical ministry development. The program emphasizes character formation, prayer discipline, and strategic leadership skills, ensuring that every woman is equipped to serve with excellence and integrity.

Through interactive sessions, group discussions, and one‑on‑one mentorship, women will discover their God‑given identity, sharpen their gifts, and be empowered to advance the Kingdom in their local communities and nations.

Topics Covered

  • Chosen vs. Called
  • Sonship as Women
  • Mandates, Mantles, and Anointing
  • The Storms Within
  • Shunammite & Deborah Syndrome
  • Intuition vs. Prophecy
  • Delays in Manifestation
  • Her Marriage
  • Addicted to God or Ministry
  • Building According to Pattern
  • End‑Time Tools for Evangelism
  • Strategic Ministry Skills
  • Subduing the Feminism Culture Within
  • Bridging the Gaps
  • The Minister’s Wife — roles of the Prophet’s, Apostle’s, Pastor’s, Evangelist’s, and Teacher’s Wives

Program Fees

Individual: $120 USD

Group (5+ women): $80 USD per person

Scholarships may be available for women serving in rural or underserved communities. Our heart is to ensure that finances never hinder a woman from stepping into her calling.
